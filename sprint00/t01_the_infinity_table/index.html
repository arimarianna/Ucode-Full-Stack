The Infinity Stones

Name Color Powers and capabilities Image
Soul Orange Allows the user to steal, control, manipulate, and alter living and dead souls. The Soul Stone also acts as a gateway to an idyllic pocket universe. To the full extent, with the support of the Power Stone, the Soul Stone gives the user control over all life in the universe.
Time Green Allows the user to look into the past and future, stop, slow down, speed up or reverse the flow of time; to travel in time; change the past and the future; beings get older and older, and trap people or entire universes into endless time loops. Fully backed by the Power Stone, the Time Stone grants the user omniscience and complete control over the past, present, and future.
Space Blue Allows the user to exist anywhere; move any object anywhere throughout reality; warp or rearrange space; teleport yourself and others; increase your speed and change the distance between objects contrary to the laws of physics. Fully backed by Power Gem, Space Gem provides the user with omnipresence.
Mind Yellow Allows the user to enhance their mental and psionic abilities and access the thoughts and dreams of other beings. At full potential, with the support of the Power Stone, the Mind Stone can access all existing minds at the same time. The Mind Stone is also a manifestation of the universal subconscious.
Reality Red Allows the user to fulfill their wishes, even if they are in direct conflict with scientific laws, and to do things that would normally be impossible. To its fullest extent, with the support of the other five Stones, the Reality Stone allows the user to alter reality on a universal scale, as well as create any type of alternate reality the user desires.
Power Purple Allows the user to access and manipulate all forms of energy; increase their physical strength and endurance; enhance any superhuman abilities; and enhance the effects of the remaining five Gems. At full potential, Power Gem gives the user omnipotence
